Welcome to Whangarei

Whangarei is my home town. Technically I didn't live in Whangarei for that long, but I to school there all my life, I would even travel 45 mins just to get to school. I know that town like the back of my hand.
Whangarei isn't a big city, but its just bigger than a town so its a city. It was a harbor in the downtown area and has one of the coolest playgrounds ever.
